About Tiny Messy Hands

Tiny Messy Hands is a local playgroup focused on providing fun, stimulating messy play sessions for children aged 0–5 years. Each session is packed with sensory-rich, hands-on activities that encourage creativity, exploration, and early childhood development.

Our play experiences are carefully themed and designed to support learning through play. Parents and caregivers attend alongside their children, helping to create a warm, inclusive, and supportive environment where everyone is welcome—and a little mess is celebrated.
